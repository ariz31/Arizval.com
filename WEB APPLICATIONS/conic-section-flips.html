Circle: Standard Form

$$ (x-h)^2 + (y-k)^2 = r^2 $$
Click to flip

Parameters

  • Center $(h, k)$: The fixed point from which all points on the circle are equidistant.
  • Radius $r$: The distance from the center to any point on the circle.

Circle: Properties

$$ A = \pi r^2 $$ $$ C = 2\pi r $$
Click to flip

Definitions

  • Area (A): The space enclosed by the circle.
  • Circumference (C): The distance around the circle.
  • Diameter (d): A line segment through the center, with endpoints on the circle. $d=2r$.

Parabola: Vertical Axis

$$ (x-h)^2 = 4p(y-k) $$
Click to flip

Parameters

  • Vertex $(h,k)$: The turning point of the parabola.
  • Focus: $(h, k+p)$
  • Directrix: $y = k-p$
  • Note: Opens up if $p>0$, down if $p<0$.

Parabola: Horizontal Axis

$$ (y-k)^2 = 4p(x-h) $$
Click to flip

Parameters

  • Vertex $(h,k)$: The turning point.
  • Focus: $(h+p, k)$
  • Directrix: $x = h-p$
  • Note: Opens right if $p>0$, left if $p<0$.

Ellipse: Horizontal Axis

$$ \frac{(x-h)^2}{a^2} + \frac{(y-k)^2}{b^2} = 1 $$
Click to flip

Parameters

  • Center: $(h,k)$
  • Vertices: $(h \pm a, k)$
  • Foci: $(h \pm c, k)$

Ellipse: Vertical Axis

$$ \frac{(x-h)^2}{b^2} + \frac{(y-k)^2}{a^2} = 1 $$
Click to flip

Parameters

  • Center: $(h,k)$
  • Vertices: $(h, k \pm a)$
  • Foci: $(h, k \pm c)$

Ellipse: Relationships

$$ c^2 = a^2 - b^2 $$
Click to flip

Definitions

  • $a$: Distance from center to a vertex.
  • $b$: Distance from center to a co-vertex.
  • $c$: Distance from center to a focus.
  • Eccentricity: $e = c/a$, where $0 < e < 1$. It measures how 'squashed' the ellipse is. A value of 0 is a perfect circle.

Hyperbola: Horizontal Axis

$$ \frac{(x-h)^2}{a^2} - \frac{(y-k)^2}{b^2} = 1 $$
Click to flip

Parameters

  • Center: $(h,k)$
  • Vertices: $(h \pm a, k)$
  • Asymptotes: $y-k = \pm \frac{b}{a}(x-h)$

Hyperbola: Vertical Axis

$$ \frac{(y-k)^2}{a^2} - \frac{(x-h)^2}{b^2} = 1 $$
Click to flip

Parameters

  • Center: $(h,k)$
  • Vertices: $(h, k \pm a)$
  • Asymptotes: $y-k = \pm \frac{a}{b}(x-h)$

Hyperbola: Relationships

$$ c^2 = a^2 + b^2 $$
Click to flip

Definitions

  • $a$: Distance from center to a vertex.
  • $c$: Distance from center to a focus.
  • Eccentricity: $e = c/a$, where $e > 1$. It measures how 'open' the hyperbola's branches are.

Identifying Conics

$$ B^2 - 4AC $$
Click to flip

Using the Discriminant

From $Ax^2+Bxy+Cy^2+Dx+Ey+F=0$

  • If $B^2 - 4AC < 0 \rightarrow$ Ellipse or Circle
  • If $B^2 - 4AC = 0 \rightarrow$ Parabola
  • If $B^2 - 4AC > 0 \rightarrow$ Hyperbola
